Changing Settings

In order to change the settings go over to the gear icon labeled with the word "Settings" in the header of the page and click it.
  • Camera detail. The image, once taken from the camera, is saved at the quality referenced here.
  • Mirror image. When enabled it alters the camera to be mirrored, it is on by default.
  • Enable auto download. When enabled whenever a user takes a picture it is automatically downloaded to the device.

Taking Pictures

To take a picture first ensure that you are on the correct page, that being the one labeled with a camera icon and the word "Camera." After ensuring that you are on the correct page, click the camera icon immediately underneath the live video feed coming from your webcam. Additionally, next to the camera are the switch input and fullscreen buttons to the left and right respectively.

Shortcuts
